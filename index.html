Research

Infrastructure Management · Inspection Planning · Decision Making under Uncertainty

Research Overview

My doctoral research focuses on developing quantitative frameworks for the optimised maintenance and inspection of civil infrastructure systems. Working within the Chair of Infrastructure Management at ETH Zürich, I combine probabilistic modelling, reliability theory, and operations research to support evidence-based asset management decisions.
